BDO Info — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

BDO Info ("the app", package org.okimasha.bdoinfo) is a free, unofficial companion app for the game Black Desert Online. It is not affiliated with Pearl Abyss. This policy explains what data the app does — and does not — handle.

Data we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences (which bosses to be notified about, lead times, quiet hours) are stored locally on your device only. They never leave your phone and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

Network connections

The app connects to BDO Alerts (api.bdoalerts.net), a third-party community service, to display live boss timers, server reset countdowns, and in-game event information. These are standard internet requests: like any web server, BDO Alerts may see your IP address as part of routine connection handling. No personal information or identifiers are sent by the app. See the BDO Alerts website for their practices.

Permissions

  • Notifications — used solely to alert you before boss spawns. Alerts are computed and scheduled entirely on your device.
  • Alarms & reminders (exact alarms) — used so spawn alerts fire on time.
  • Internet — used only for the live data described above.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
