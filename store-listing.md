## Full description (copy exactly)

```
BDO Info is an unofficial companion app for Black Desert Online (NA) that makes
sure you never miss a world boss again.

⚔️ BOSS TIMERS
Every upcoming world boss spawn with live countdowns — Kzarka, Karanda, Kutum,
Nouver, Garmoth, Vell, Offin, Uturi, Sangoon, Bulgasal, Quint, Muraka, and the
Golden Pig King. All times shown in your local timezone.

🔔 NOTIFICATIONS THAT ACTUALLY FIRE
Reliable alarms that work offline and survive reboots. Pick which bosses you
care about, set multiple reminder times per boss (5 to 60 minutes before
spawn), and define quiet hours per day of the week so weeknight sleep stays
sacred while weekend grinding stays loud.

📅 WEEKLY SCHEDULE
The full 7-day spawn schedule at a glance, with the next spawn highlighted.

💎 LOOT TABLES
Tap any boss to see its notable drops with real item icons and community-
estimated drop rates — honestly labeled, since official rates aren't published.

📡 LIVE SERVER DATA
Live boss countdowns, daily and weekly reset timers, and Golden Pig Cave
status, powered by the BDO Alerts community service. If you're offline, the
built-in schedule keeps everything working.

No account. No ads. No data collected. Free.

BDO Info is a fan-made app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Pearl
Abyss. Black Desert Online is a trademark of Pearl Abyss Corp. Live data
courtesy of bdoalerts.net; item data referenced from bdocodex.com.
```
